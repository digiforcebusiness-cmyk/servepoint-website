Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

1. Introduction

ServePoint ("we", "our", or "us") operates the ServePoint POS mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our app.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following data to provide and improve our service:

  • Account information: Email address and display name when you register or sign in with Google.
  • Business data: Orders, products, categories, and sales data you enter into the app. This data is stored locally on your device and optionally synced to Firebase Firestore.
  • Usage data: App crash reports and basic usage analytics to improve performance.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and maintain the app's functionality
  • To sync your data across multiple devices (Pro feature)
  • To manage your subscription via RevenueCat
  • To send important app-related notifications

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services which have their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication and real-time database sync
  • RevenueCat: Subscription and in-app purchase management
  • Google Sign-In: Optional authentication method

5. Data Storage

Your business data (orders, products, sales) is stored locally on your device using SQLite. With an active Pro subscription, data is also synced to Firebase Firestore servers located in the United States.

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Firebase data is secured using Firebase Security Rules requiring authentication.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To request data deletion, contact us at digiforcebusiness@gmail.com.

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
