Quick troubleshooting

  • No engine sound on iPhone? Check that the silent / ring switch is off, then turn the media volume up while SuperRev is open.
  • No speed-based revs? The engine note also reacts to motion and the on-screen throttle pedal even without GPS. To enable GPS, go to Settings → SuperRev → Location and choose While Using the App.
  • Bluetooth speaker not working? Pair the speaker first via iOS / Android settings, then launch SuperRev — it picks up whatever output the system has selected.
