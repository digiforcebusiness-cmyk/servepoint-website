A real engine, simulated on your phone

Not a single looping clip — layered recordings that move with you.

🏎️

Layered engine audio

Real recorded engine layers — idle, low, mid, high and redline — crossfade by RPM and blend on/off throttle, so the note is alive across the whole rev range.

📡

Driven by your sensors

GPS speed and the accelerometer feed a real-time RPM model. Accelerate, cruise, or lift off — the engine responds the way a real one would.

🎚️

Live dashboard

A full instrument cluster — tachometer, speedometer and gear indicator — that sweeps in sync with the sound. Portrait or landscape.

🔊

Hold to rev

Parked? Hold the throttle pedal and blip the engine on demand — the layers climb to redline and settle back to idle.

🚗

Swap engines

Flip through the car carousel to change engines — a high-revving rotary, a torquey inline-six, and more — each with its own character.

📴

On-device & offline

No account, no sign-up, no internet required. Location and motion stay on your phone and are never collected or shared.

How it works

Three steps from your pocket to a soundtrack for the drive.

1

Pick your engine

Choose an engine pack from the carousel — each one is a different recorded powertrain.

2

Mount & calibrate

Set your phone in its mount and tap the axis that points forward, so motion sensing matches the car.

3

Drive

Pull away and the engine note rises with your speed and throttle. Lift off and it settles. That's it.

Free to download

SuperRev is free on Google Play. No sign-up, no account — install it, pick an engine, and drive.
