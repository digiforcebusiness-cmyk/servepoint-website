The Short Version

SuperRev turns your phone into a real-time engine-sound simulator. It reads your phone's GPS speed and motion sensors to estimate a simulated engine RPM, and plays layered engine audio that responds to how you drive — all on your device. SuperRev has no accounts and runs no servers of its own. The app displays a banner advertisement supplied by Google AdMob; to serve those ads, Google's advertising SDK collects certain device and usage information as described below. Apart from advertising, SuperRev does not collect personal data, and it has no analytics or other third-party tracking.

1. Who We Are

SuperRev ("the app", "we", "our") is a mobile engine-sound simulator app published by YOUNESS HARNAFI / DIGIFORCE LLC. This Privacy Policy explains what data the app does and does not handle when you use it on Android or iOS.

2. Location Data

SuperRev requests location access because the simulated engine RPM is driven partly by how fast you are moving. The phone's GPS speed is read continuously while the app is running and is used, in real time, to calculate the engine sound you hear.

  • Location used for the engine simulation is processed entirely on your device. It is read by the operating system's location service, used immediately to compute speed and RPM, and then discarded. It is never written to disk as a history, and SuperRev never transmits it to us or stores it on any server.
  • Separately, the Google AdMob advertising SDK (Section 6) may infer your approximate location — for example from your IP address — to help select relevant ads. That advertising-related processing is carried out by Google, not by SuperRev.

If you deny or revoke location permission, the app still works — the engine simulation then relies on the motion sensors and the in-app throttle control.

3. Motion & Other Sensors

SuperRev reads the device's accelerometer (and related motion sensors) to detect acceleration, braking, and the orientation of the phone. This lets the app sense "throttle" — for example, a forward jolt of the phone revs the simulated engine — and calibrate which way the vehicle is pointing.

Sensor readings are used in real time to compute the engine sound and are immediately discarded. They are not recorded, not stored, and not transmitted.

4. Engine Audio

SuperRev plays engine sound through an audio engine embedded in the app. Audio is rendered entirely on-device from sound files bundled inside the app and played through your phone's speaker or any connected Bluetooth / wired output you choose. The audio engine processes no personal data and makes no network connections.

5. Data Stored Only on Your Device

The app saves a small amount of information locally so your setup persists between sessions. None of it is transmitted off your device:

  • Selected car — which engine pack is currently active
  • Calibration — the forward-axis orientation you set so motion sensing matches how the phone is mounted
  • Preferences — app settings and toggles

This data is stored in the device's local app storage. You can clear it at any time by uninstalling the app.

6. Advertising — Google AdMob

SuperRev displays a banner advertisement using Google AdMob, Google's mobile advertising service. To select, deliver, measure, and limit the frequency of ads, the Google Mobile Ads SDK embedded in the app collects and transmits certain information to Google. This typically includes:

  • An advertising identifier (the Android Advertising ID or iOS IDFA) — a resettable, per-device ID used for advertising
  • Device information — such as device model, operating-system version, language, and screen characteristics
  • Approximate location — typically derived from your IP address (not your GPS coordinates)
  • Ad-interaction data — whether an ad was shown, viewed, or tapped

This information is collected and used by Google, acting as an independent third party, and is governed by Google's own policies — see the Google Privacy Policy and How Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services. SuperRev itself does not receive your advertising identifier, your contact details, or your precise location from this process.

You can limit ad personalization at any time: on Android via Settings → Privacy → Ads (where you can also delete or reset your advertising ID), and on iOS via Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking and Apple Advertising. Resetting or deleting the advertising ID, or opting out of personalization, does not remove ads but makes them non-personalized.

Aside from AdMob, SuperRev contains no analytics SDKs, no measurement or attribution SDKs, and no other third-party tracking technologies.

7. Permissions the App Requests

The app requests only the permissions needed for the features it provides:

  • Location (precise & approximate) — to read GPS speed for the RPM simulation (Section 2)
  • Body / motion sensors & high-sampling-rate sensors — to read the accelerometer for throttle and orientation sensing (Section 3)
  • Internet — used by the Google AdMob SDK to load advertisements (Section 6)
  • Advertising ID — declared so the Google Mobile Ads SDK can serve ads (Section 6)

The app does not request access to your contacts, calendar, microphone, camera, photos, or SMS, and the engine simulation runs only while the app is in the foreground.

8. Data We Do Not Collect

SuperRev itself operates no servers that store user data. Other than the advertising data handled by Google AdMob (Section 6), we do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Names, email addresses, or phone numbers (the app has no sign-up or account)
  • Your GPS coordinates, routes, or location history
  • Motion-sensor recordings or driving history
  • App usage analytics, telemetry, or crash reports gathered by us
  • Contacts, photos, calendar, microphone, or camera data

9. Children's Privacy

SuperRev is not directed to children under 13 and is intended for a general audience aged 13 and over. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because the app shows advertising, it is not enrolled in Google Play's "Designed for Families" program. Parents and guardians with questions can contact us at the email below.

10. Your Rights

SuperRev holds no personal information about you on any server, so there is nothing for us to access, correct, or delete remotely. You retain full control over:

  • On-device data — uninstall the app to wipe everything it has stored locally
  • Location and sensor access — toggle in Android Settings → Apps → SuperRev → Permissions, or iOS Settings → SuperRev
  • Advertising — reset or delete your advertising ID and opt out of ad personalization in your device's privacy settings (Section 6)

For advertising data held by Google, you may exercise your rights directly with Google under the Google Privacy Policy. Users in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, California, and other jurisdictions with applicable privacy laws may also contact us with any request; because we hold no personal data ourselves, most such requests will be answered confirming that no data about you exists with us.

11. Data Retention

We retain no user data because SuperRev collects none on its own servers. Location and sensor readings exist only momentarily in device memory while computing engine sound. On-device preferences persist only as long as the app remains installed. Advertising data collected by Google AdMob is retained by Google under Google's own retention policies.

12. International Users

SuperRev is available worldwide. The app itself does not transfer personal data across borders. Advertising requests made by the Google AdMob SDK may be served by Google's global infrastructure and are subject to Google's own privacy practices.
