Privacy Policy

SuperRev — Engine Sounds

Last updated: May 16, 2026

The Short Version

SuperRev turns your phone into a real-time engine-sound simulator. It reads your phone's GPS speed and motion sensors to estimate a simulated engine RPM, and plays layered engine audio that responds to how you drive. All of this happens on your device. SuperRev has no accounts, runs no servers, and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. There is no advertising, no analytics, and no third-party tracking.

1. Who We Are

SuperRev ("the app", "we", "our") is a mobile engine-sound simulator app published by YOUNESS HARNAFI / DIGIFORCE LLC. This Privacy Policy explains what data the app does and does not handle when you use it on Android or iOS.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not operate any servers that store user data. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Names, email addresses, or phone numbers (the app has no sign-up or account)
  • Your GPS coordinates, routes, or location history
  • Motion-sensor recordings or driving history
  • App usage analytics, telemetry, or crash reports
  • Behavioral or interest profiles
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Contacts, photos, calendar, microphone, or camera data

The app contains no third-party advertising, no analytics SDKs, and no tracking pixels.

3. Location Data

SuperRev requests location access because the simulated engine RPM is driven partly by how fast you are moving. The phone's GPS speed is read continuously while the app is running and is used, in real time, to calculate the engine sound you hear.

  • Location is processed entirely on your device. It is read by the operating system's location service, used immediately to compute speed and RPM, and then discarded. The next reading replaces it.
  • Location is never written to disk, never stored as a history, and never transmitted to us or to any third party.
  • SuperRev declares the background-location and foreground-service permissions so the engine simulation can keep running while the app is mounted in a vehicle and the screen is off or the app is in the background — for example while it is the active "instrument" during a drive. Even in this mode, location is only ever used on-device to keep the RPM simulation accurate; nothing is recorded or sent.

If you deny or revoke location permission, the app still works — the engine simulation then relies on the motion sensors alone (see Section 4) and on the in-app throttle control.

4. Motion & Other Sensors

SuperRev reads the device's accelerometer (and related motion sensors) to detect acceleration, braking, and the orientation of the phone. This lets the app sense "throttle" — for example, a forward jolt of the phone revs the simulated engine — and calibrate which way the vehicle is pointing.

Sensor readings are used in real time to compute the engine sound and are immediately discarded. They are not recorded, not stored, and not transmitted. The app may request the high-sampling-rate sensors permission so the motion response feels responsive; this changes only the sensor update rate, not what is done with the data.

5. Engine Audio

SuperRev plays engine sound through FMOD, an audio engine by Firelight Technologies Pty Ltd, embedded in the app. Audio is rendered entirely on-device from sound files bundled inside the app and played through your phone's speaker or any connected Bluetooth / wired output you choose. The audio engine processes no personal data and makes no network connections.

6. Data Stored Only on Your Device

The app saves a small amount of information locally so your setup persists between sessions. None of it is transmitted off your device:

  • Selected car — which engine pack is currently active
  • Calibration — the forward-axis orientation you set so motion sensing matches how the phone is mounted
  • Preferences — app settings and toggles

This data is stored using the device's local app storage. You can clear it at any time by uninstalling the app.

7. Permissions the App Requests

The app requests only the permissions needed for the features it provides:

  • Location (precise & approximate) — to read GPS speed for the RPM simulation (Section 3)
  • Background location — so the simulation can continue while the app runs in the background as a vehicle instrument (Section 3)
  • Foreground service — to keep the simulation and audio running reliably while the app is active
  • Body / motion sensors & high-sampling-rate sensors — to read the accelerometer for throttle and orientation sensing (Section 4)

The app does not request access to your contacts, calendar, microphone, camera, photos, or SMS.

8. No Advertising, No Analytics

This version of SuperRev contains no advertising networks, no analytics or measurement SDKs, and no tracking technologies. No advertising identifier is read or used. If a future version of the app introduces advertising or analytics, this Privacy Policy will be updated before that version is released, and the app's Google Play and App Store data-safety disclosures will be updated to match.

9. Children's Privacy

SuperRev is not directed to children under 13. The app does not collect personal information from any user. Parents and guardians who have questions about the app can contact us at the email below.

10. Your Rights

Because we do not collect personal information on any server, there is nothing about you for us to access, correct, or delete remotely. You retain full control over:

  • On-device data — uninstall the app to wipe everything it has stored locally
  • Location access — toggle in Android Settings → Apps → SuperRev → Permissions, or iOS Settings → SuperRev
  • Sensor access — managed by the same OS permission screens

Users in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, California, and other jurisdictions with privacy laws granting rights of access, correction, deletion, and objection may exercise those rights by contacting us. Because we hold no personal data, most requests will be answered confirming that no data about you exists.

11. Data Retention

We do not retain any user data because we do not collect any. Location and sensor readings exist only momentarily in device memory while they are being used to compute engine sound. On-device preferences persist only as long as the app remains installed.

12. International Users

SuperRev is available worldwide. The app does not transfer personal data across borders because it does not collect or transmit personal data. All processing happens locally on your device.
