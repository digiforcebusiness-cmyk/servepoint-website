Delete Your BrainHook Account

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time.

How to Delete Your Account In-App

The easiest way to delete your account is directly inside the BrainHook app:

1 Open the BrainHook app and sign in.
2 Tap the Settings tab at the bottom of the screen.
3 Scroll down and tap Delete Account (shown in red).
4 Confirm the deletion in the dialog that appears.
⚠️ This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

What Gets Deleted

  • Your account profile (display name, email, subscription status)
  • All mnemonics saved in your library
  • Your Firebase Authentication account

Deletion is immediate and permanent. We do not retain any personal data after your account is deleted.

What Is Not Deleted

  • Anonymous feedback submissions that are not linked to a deletable identifier (retained for product improvement)
  • Transaction records held by Google Play or Apple App Store (managed by the respective platform)
