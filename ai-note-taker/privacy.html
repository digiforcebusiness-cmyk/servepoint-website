Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 22, 2026

Speech to Text Note Taker ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and the choices you have. It applies to the Speech to Text Note Taker mobile app and the companion web portal.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: Email address and display name when you sign in with Google, Apple or email.
  • Audio recordings: Audio you record stays on your device. If you opt into cloud transcription (Premium), the audio is uploaded to your private Firebase Storage bucket for processing.
  • Transcripts and summaries: Stored locally on your device. If you sign in, metadata syncs to your private Firebase Firestore so the web portal can show your notes.
  • Custom vocabulary: Words and phrases you add to bias transcription accuracy.
  • Calendar data: When you connect Google Calendar or Microsoft Outlook, we fetch upcoming event titles, times and locations on demand. Calendar data is never stored on our servers — it lives in memory only while the Calendar tab is open.
  • Usage metrics: Cloud-minutes consumed in the current month (for fair-use enforcement) and device push-notification tokens.

How We Use Your Information

  • To record, transcribe and summarise your audio.
  • To answer questions about your notes when you use AI Chat.
  • To show your upcoming meetings inside the app when you connect a calendar.
  • To send push notifications when a transcript or summary is ready.
  • To enforce the Premium fair-use cap.
  • To process subscription payments through Google Play / Apple App Store.

Google API Services User Data Policy

Speech to Text Note Taker's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs to any other app will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Specifically, we request the calendar.readonly scope so you can see upcoming meetings in the app and tap one to start recording. We only read event titles, times and locations. We do not modify, create or delete calendar entries, we do not transfer calendar data to any third party, we do not use calendar data for advertising, and we do not allow humans to read your calendar data unless you give explicit consent for support purposes.

Microsoft Graph Permissions

If you connect Outlook, we use the Calendars.Read scope to fetch your upcoming events for the same purpose. The OAuth tokens are stored only on your device. Calendar data is never persisted to our servers.

Data Storage & Retention

  • Audio, transcripts and summaries are stored on your device by default and remain there until you delete them.
  • If you sign in and use cloud transcription, audio is uploaded to your private Firebase Storage bucket under users/<your uid>/uploads/ and is automatically removed after transcription completes.
  • Cloud-generated transcripts and summaries are synced to your private Firestore document under users/<your uid>/notes/.
  • OAuth refresh tokens for Outlook are stored on-device only.
  • You can request full deletion of your cloud data at any time by emailing us.

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, Firestore database, Cloud Storage, Cloud Functions, Cloud Messaging.
  • OpenAI: Cloud Whisper transcription and GPT-4o-mini summary / AI Chat. OpenAI processes audio and text under their API data usage policy and does not retain content for training.
  • RevenueCat: Subscription receipt validation and entitlement state.
  • Google Calendar API: Read-only access to your calendar events.
  • Microsoft Graph: Read-only access to your Outlook calendar (optional).
  • Apple: Sign in with Apple, App Store payments (iOS only).

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing us at digiforcebusiness@gmail.com. You can also disconnect calendar integrations from inside the app at any time.

Children's Privacy

Speech to Text Note Taker is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be notified in-app or by email. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at digiforcebusiness@gmail.com.
