Frequently Asked Questions

Does the app work offline?
Yes. Every recording is captured on-device and transcribed locally with Whisper before any cloud step. Premium adds higher-accuracy cloud transcription, but offline transcripts are always saved first.
Which languages are supported?
The on-device model is English. Premium users get cloud transcription that auto-detects 50+ languages, and summaries are written in the same language as the recording.
What is the fair-use cap?
Premium includes ~20 hours of cloud-enhanced transcription per month. After the cap, recordings keep saving on-device with the free on-device transcriber — there is never a hard wall.
How do I connect my calendar?
Open the app's Calendar tab and tap Connect on Google or Outlook. Grant the read-only scope on the consent screen. Events show up immediately. You can disconnect from the same tab any time.
Why does Google show "Google hasn't verified this app"?
The app is currently in test mode with Google. Tap Advanced → Go to Speech to Text Note Taker (unsafe) to proceed — it's safe; the warning only appears because Google's formal verification review is ongoing. Once verified the warning disappears for everyone.
Where are my recordings stored?
Audio and transcripts live on your device. If you sign in, metadata syncs to your private Firebase project so the web portal can show your notes too. Nobody else can read them.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Open the Google Play Store → Subscriptions, find Speech to Text Note Taker, and cancel. Your Premium features stay active until the end of the billing period.
Can I edit the transcript?
Yes. Tap any segment in the Transcript tab to edit it. Your edits are preserved when you re-transcribe in the cloud.
How do I delete a recording permanently?
Long-press a note on the Home tab or open it and use More → Delete recording — it moves to Trash. From Account → Trash you can restore it, or tap Delete forever to remove it permanently along with its audio file.
Is my data ever used to train AI models?
No. Cloud transcription is processed through OpenAI's API under their no-training-on-API-data policy. We do not sell, share, or use your recordings for any model training.
