Frequently asked questions

How do I add services and categories?

Open the Store tab → tap New Service (or the Categories tab → New Category). Adding and editing the service catalog requires a Pro subscription.

How does the Pro subscription work?

ServePoint Spa Pro is an auto-renewing subscription — $9.99/month or $59.99/year. Payment is charged to your Apple ID or Google Play account at confirmation. It renews automatically unless you turn off auto-renew at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.

How do I cancel my subscription?
  • iPhone/iPad: Settings → tap your name → Subscriptions → ServePoint Spa → Cancel.
  • Android: Google Play → Profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → ServePoint Spa → Cancel.

Deleting the app does not cancel a subscription — cancel through the store.

How do I delete my account?

In the app: Settings → Delete Account. This permanently removes your account and associated data. You can also request deletion by emailing us.

How do I sign in?

Use email & password, Continue with Google, or Continue with Apple. Your data syncs across all your staff's devices in real time.

Does the app work offline?

Yes. ServePoint Spa is offline-first — you can take orders, manage appointments and print receipts without an internet connection. Data syncs automatically when you're back online.

Which printers are supported?

Bluetooth thermal receipt printers (58 mm / 80 mm). Pair the printer in your device's Bluetooth settings, then select it in the app.
