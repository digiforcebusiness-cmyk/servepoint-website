Privacy Policy

Teleprompter for Video

Last updated: May 14, 2026

The Short Version

Teleprompter for Video does not collect personal data or track usage. Your scripts stay on your phone. Voice sync runs on-device. The camera and microphone are used only while a recording is active, and recordings save straight to your Photos library — not to any server. Cloud sync is opt-in and limited to a hidden Google Drive folder only this app can read. Subscriptions are processed by Apple and Google; receipts are validated by RevenueCat. No advertising, no analytics, no third-party tracking.

1. Who We Are

Teleprompter for Video ("the app", "we", "our") is a mobile teleprompter and video recording app published by YOUNESS HARNAFI. This Privacy Policy explains what data the app does and does not handle when you use it on iOS or Android.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not operate any servers that store user data. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Names, email addresses, or phone numbers (the app has no sign-up)
  • Voice recordings or transcripts beyond on-device speech recognition (see Section 4)
  • Recorded videos (they save to your Photos library only)
  • Location data
  • App usage analytics, telemetry, or crash reports
  • Behavioral or interest profiles
  • Advertising identifiers

The app contains no third-party advertising, no analytics SDKs, and no tracking pixels.

3. Data Stored Only on Your Device

The app saves the following information locally so your work persists between sessions. None of it is transmitted off your device by us:

  • Scripts — title, body text, folder, timestamps
  • Recording sessions — duration, pace, session date (for the Analytics screen)
  • Preferences — selected font, text size, line spacing, scroll speed, countdown duration, keep-screen-on toggle, theme
  • Bookmarks — sections you mark with [INTRO], [BREAK], [Q&A]

This data is stored using the device's local storage (Hive). You can clear it at any time by deleting scripts and sessions individually inside the app, or by uninstalling the app entirely.

4. Microphone & Voice Sync

The app requests microphone access so the teleprompter can scroll in sync with your speech. The recognition uses the platform's built-in speech APIs:

  • iOS: Apple's Speech framework (SFSpeechRecognizer). On modern iPhones this runs entirely on-device. On older devices Apple may transmit short audio segments to its servers for transcription — this is governed by Apple's own privacy policy and is not something the app can intercept or store.
  • Android: Android's SpeechRecognizer service. Depending on the device manufacturer and OS version, this may use Google's cloud speech service. The app sends nothing additional alongside the audio.

The app does not store audio recordings, does not log transcriptions, and does not send your voice anywhere of our choosing. The transcription is read and immediately used to advance the script highlight; the underlying audio buffer is discarded once recognition completes.

Voice sync is optional. The teleprompter works fully without microphone access — Manual mode auto-scrolls at a fixed speed you control.

5. Camera & Recordings

The app requests camera (and optionally microphone) access when you start a video recording session. The camera feed is captured only while the recording is active. Stopping the recording produces an MP4 file that is saved to your device's Photos library at your discretion.

Recorded videos are never uploaded to any server by this app. Sharing a recording (via the system share sheet, AirDrop, social apps, etc.) is initiated by you and governed by the destination service's own privacy policy.

Free users see a small "Teleprompter for Video" watermark composited into the bottom-right of recorded videos. Pro subscribers do not. The watermark compositing happens entirely on-device during the recording-finalize step — no video data is sent off-device for this.

6. Optional: Google Drive Sync

If you tap "Connect Google Drive" in Settings, the app uses Google Sign-In with the drive.appdata OAuth scope. This is the most restrictive Drive scope Google offers:

  • The app can read and write a single file scripts.json inside a hidden per-app folder that Google calls the "AppData folder"
  • The app cannot read, write, or even see any of your other Drive files
  • You can revoke access at any time via your Google Account's permissions page

Only the scripts you've saved in the app are synced. Recordings, audio, settings, and analytics are never uploaded. The sync file lives in your own Drive account, not on any server we control.

If you sign out of Google Drive in Settings, the local copy of your scripts remains untouched and future edits stop syncing until you reconnect.

7. Subscriptions & In-App Purchases

The optional Pro subscription (Monthly, Annual, or Lifetime) is processed by the platform's standard in-app purchase system:

  • iOS: Apple's StoreKit / App Store. Apple receives your payment information; we do not see your card details.
  • Android: Google Play Billing. Google receives your payment information; we do not see your card details.

To verify your subscription status and unlock the watermark-free recording benefit, the app uses RevenueCat, a subscription-management service. RevenueCat receives a randomly generated anonymous user ID, your purchase receipt from Apple or Google, and basic device information (device model, OS version, locale, app version). RevenueCat does not receive your name, email address, payment details, or any of your scripts / recordings.

RevenueCat's own privacy policy is available at revenuecat.com/privacy.

8. Permissions the App Requests

The app requests only the permissions needed for the features you actually use:

  • Microphone — when you enable Voice Sync or record a video with audio
  • Camera — when you start a video recording session
  • Speech Recognition (iOS) — to transcribe spoken words to the teleprompter highlight
  • Photos library / Add to library — to save recorded videos
  • Internet — required only for optional Google Drive Sync and RevenueCat receipt validation. Voice sync and recording work fully offline.
  • Documents folder — to import .txt or .md scripts you've saved in Files
  • Display over other apps (Android only) — for Floating Mode, which puts a small teleprompter window on top of other apps

The app does not request access to your location, contacts, calendar, or any other sensitive data.

9. Children's Privacy

Teleprompter for Video is not directed to children under 13. The app does not collect personal information from any user, but the camera-recording, microphone, and subscription features are not intended for children's use without adult supervision. Parents and guardians who believe their child has used the app and wish to ask any questions can contact us at the email below.

10. Your Rights

Because we do not collect personal information on our servers, there is nothing about you for us to access, correct, or delete remotely. You retain full control over:

  • On-device scripts and sessions — delete individually inside the app, or uninstall to wipe everything
  • Google Drive Sync — disconnect in Settings, or revoke at myaccount.google.com/permissions
  • Subscription — manage or cancel via Settings → Subscriptions on iOS, or Google Play → Subscriptions on Android
  • Microphone or camera access — toggle in iOS Settings → Teleprompter for Video, or Android Settings → Apps → Teleprompter for Video → Permissions

Users in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, California, and other jurisdictions with privacy laws granting rights of access, correction, deletion, and objection may exercise those rights at any time by contacting us. Because we hold no personal data, most requests will be answered confirming that no data exists.

11. Data Retention

We do not retain any user data because we do not collect any. On-device data persists only as long as the app remains installed. RevenueCat retains anonymous subscription records for as long as your subscription is active plus a retention window defined by their own policy.

12. International Users

Teleprompter for Video is available worldwide. The app does not transfer personal data across borders because it does not collect personal data. Cloud requests you initiate (Google Drive Sync, RevenueCat receipt validation, in-app purchase processing) may be served by Google's, Apple's, or RevenueCat's global infrastructure, subject to those providers' own privacy policies.
