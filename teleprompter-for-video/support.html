Getting Started

Create a script

Tap the + button on the home screen, paste or type your script, and give it a title. Wrap a word in **double asterisks** to make it bold on screen.

Voice Sync mode

Start a session and begin speaking — the teleprompter highlights and scrolls to follow your voice automatically. Speech recognition runs on your device; no recording is sent anywhere.

Manual mode

Prefer a steady auto-scroll? Manual mode scrolls at a fixed speed you control with the on-screen slider. Tapping Record starts the scroll automatically.

Frequently Asked Questions

The teleprompter isn't following my voice

  • Make sure you granted Microphone and Speech Recognition permission (iOS Settings → Teleprompter for Video).
  • Pick the language that matches your script in Settings → Voice Recognition.
  • Speech recognition needs a reasonably quiet environment — heavy background noise can break tracking.

How do I record a video?

Turn on the camera with the camera button in the session top bar, then tap the red Record button. The teleprompter scrolls while you record. Tap it again to stop — the clip saves to your photo library.

There's a watermark on my recording

Free recordings carry a small "Teleprompter for Video" badge in the bottom-right corner. Upgrading to Pro removes it — every recording exports clean. All other features are free.

What does Pro include?

Pro's single benefit is watermark-free recording. It's available as a Monthly, Annual, or Lifetime purchase. Manage or cancel a subscription anytime in iOS Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions.

How do I restore a purchase?

Open the app's paywall screen and tap Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

What is the floating window?

On iPhone, the floating window pops the script into a small Picture-in-Picture panel that stays on top of other apps. Tap the picture-in-picture icon in the session top bar to open it.

Does the app collect my data?

No personal data is collected. Scripts and settings stay on your device. Google Drive sync is optional and limited to a hidden app folder. Full details are in our Privacy Policy.

How do I reset the app?

Open Settings → Data. "Reset Settings" restores defaults without touching your scripts; "Delete All Data" removes scripts, sessions, and settings.

Still Stuck?

If none of the above solved it, email us at digiforcebusiness@gmail.com with your device model, iOS version, and a short description of what happened. Screenshots or a screen recording help a lot.
