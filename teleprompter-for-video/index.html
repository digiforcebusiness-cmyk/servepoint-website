Everything you need to film a clean take

No earpiece, no memorizing, no awkward pauses.

🎙️

Voice sync

The script highlights and scrolls to follow your speech as you talk. Pause to think and it waits for you. Speech recognition runs entirely on your device.

🎬

Built-in recording

Film straight from the app with the front or back camera while the teleprompter scrolls. Your clip saves right to your photo library.

🪟

Floating window

Pop the script into a movable Picture-in-Picture panel that stays on top while you record in another camera app.

⏱️

Manual auto-scroll

Prefer a steady pace? Manual mode scrolls at a speed you set, with a countdown so you have time to get into position.

🎨

Made to read

Adjust text size, font, line spacing, and mirror mode for beam-splitter rigs. Bold your key words so you never miss an emphasis.

☁️

Optional cloud sync

Back up your scripts to your own Google Drive. It's opt-in, limited to a hidden app folder, and off by default.

How it works

Three steps from blank page to finished take.

1

Add your script

Paste or type your script and give it a title. Highlight the words you want to emphasize.

2

Start a session

Pick Voice Sync or Manual mode, turn on the camera, and position your phone.

3

Hit record and talk

The teleprompter follows you while you record. Stop when you're done — the video is in your library.

Free to use

Every feature is free. Free recordings include a small corner badge — an optional Pro upgrade (Monthly, Annual, or Lifetime) removes it so your videos export completely clean.
