Eyes that follow you

Both eyes track your finger continuously. Tap anywhere, drag, release — the face is alive and watching.

Haptic feedback

How to play

It's a toy, not a game. There's nothing to learn.

1

Touch the face

Tap and hold anywhere on the head. The mesh starts deforming under your finger.

2

Drag in any direction

Stretch a cheek. Pull the nose. Squish the forehead. Eyes follow your finger the whole time.

3

Release and watch it bounce

Let go and the face springs back with a few satisfying oscillations. Then do it again.
