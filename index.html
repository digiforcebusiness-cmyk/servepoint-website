Features

Everything you need to run your business

Built specifically for restaurants, cafés and snack bars — ServePoint covers every step from taking an order to closing the day.

Order Management

Take orders at lightning speed

A clean, fast interface designed for real restaurant environments. Add items, modify quantities, apply discounts and send orders to the kitchen — all without missing a beat.

  • ✓ Smart category and product browsing
  • ✓ Discounts, notes and special requests
  • ✓ Split bills and multiple payment methods
  • ✓ Orders sync instantly to the kitchen display
Kitchen Display (KDS)

Real-time orders on the chef's screen

No more paper tickets, no missed orders. The kitchen display shows every order the moment it's placed, and the chef marks items as ready with a single tap.

  • ✓ Orders appear instantly after being placed
  • ✓ Mark individual items or full orders as ready
  • ✓ Works on any Android tablet or phone
  • ✓ Real-time sync between all devices in your venue
Payment & Receipts

Fast checkout, beautiful receipts

Process cash, card and mixed payments in seconds. Print thermal receipts or share digital ones — the transaction is closed before the customer puts their wallet away.

  • ✓ Cash, card and split payment modes
  • ✓ Auto change calculation
  • ✓ Bluetooth thermal printer (58mm & 80mm)
  • ✓ Stock updated automatically on payment
Inventory Control

Never run out of anything again

Track ingredient stock levels automatically as you sell. Get low-stock alerts before you run out, so your kitchen is always prepared for the rush.

  • ✓ Ingredients deducted automatically per sale
  • ✓ Low-stock alerts with configurable thresholds
  • ✓ Full ingredient and product management
  • ✓ Visual stock level indicators
Reports & Analytics

Know your numbers at a glance

Daily and monthly revenue reports with zero effort. See which products sell most, when your peak hours are and how much you've earned — all in one clean dashboard.

  • ✓ Daily and monthly revenue summaries
  • ✓ Best-selling products ranking
  • ✓ Peak hour insights
  • ✓ Export reports as PDF

Cloud Backup

All your business data is securely stored in Firebase. Switch devices, reinstall the app or add a new tablet — your data is always there.

Multi-Device Sync

Waiter on one phone, chef on a tablet — every device stays in sync in real time via Firebase.

Thermal Printing

Print receipts via Bluetooth to 58mm and 80mm thermal printers. Fast and cable-free.

4 Languages

Switch between French, English, Spanish and Arabic (RTL) instantly. Perfect for any team.
