Features

Everything you need to run your business

Built specifically for restaurants, cafés and snack bars — ServePoint covers every step from taking an order to closing the day.

Order Management

Take orders at the table or counter with a fast, intuitive interface. Add items, apply discounts, split bills, and process payments in seconds. Orders sync instantly to the kitchen.

Kitchen Display (KDS)

Real-time orders on the chef's screen. Mark items ready when done — no paper tickets, no missed orders.

Inventory Control

Track ingredient stock levels automatically. Get low-stock alerts before you run out of anything important.

Sales Reports

Daily and monthly revenue reports. See your best-selling products, peak hours and total sales at a glance.

Multi-Device Sync

Waiter takes the order on one device — it appears instantly on the kitchen screen on another. Real-time, powered by Firebase.

Works Offline

No internet? No problem. ServePoint stores everything locally first and syncs when connection is restored.

Thermal Printing

Print receipts directly to Bluetooth thermal printers (58mm and 80mm). Fast, reliable, no USB cables needed.

4 Languages Built-In

Switch between French, English, Spanish and Arabic instantly — including full right-to-left support for Arabic. Perfect for multilingual teams and international locations.
